Find Answers to Common Questions About MBBS Abroad

Admission & Eligibility

Is NEET mandatory for MBBS Abroad?

Yes. NEET qualification is required as per NMC rules. Without NEET, your MBBS degree abroad won't be valid in India.

What is the minimum NEET score required?

There is no fixed cut-off. You must be NEET qualified (even with minimum marks) to get eligibility for MBBS abroad.

Can I get admission without NEET?

No. Without NEET qualification, your MBBS degree abroad won't be valid in India as per NMC regulations.

What documents are needed for admission?

Required documents include:

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • NEET Score Card
  • Valid Passport
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Admission Application Form (we assist with this)
How do I confirm my admission?

After submitting documents and paying the first installment, the admission process starts immediately. The university issues an admission letter once all requirements are fulfilled.

What is the SAARC quota for MBBS in Bangladesh?

Around 22 students from India can get free education under the SAARC quota. Eligibility requires 99% in Class 12. Application must be submitted through the Bangladesh Embassy in Delhi.

English Tests & Process

Is Duolingo or IELTS required?

Required in some countries like Kazakhstan. We provide full preparation and registration help for these tests.

How long does the admission process take?

The admission process typically takes 3 to 6 weeks, depending on the country, university, and embassy visa approval timeline.

Validity & Safety

Is MBBS Abroad valid in India?

Yes, if the university is NMC-recognized and you clear the NEXT exam (National Exit Test) post-MBBS. We only work with NMC-recognized universities.

How to avoid frauds in MBBS admissions?

Always verify:

  • University recognition proof (NMC/WHO)
  • Invoice for every payment made
  • Direct communication with university
  • Avoid agents who promise unrealistic things
